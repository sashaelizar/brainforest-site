See connections between the science of human nature, the way we perceive the world, our origins in the universe, and how little we actually know. Nobody has it all figured out, even if they present their beliefs as facts. </span></span></p></div><div type="paragraph"></div><div><p id="viewer-6nndd"><span><span>You are bound to meet people with completely different perspectives. This can range in energy required, from small (leaving the room, blocking a number, sticking your phone in a drawer) to medium (changing who you surround yourself with, switching roommates, dorms, or campuses) to huge (switching majors, making a lifestyle decision, or traveling).
